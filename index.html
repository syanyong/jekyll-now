About me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical Engineering at King Mongkut’s Institute of Technology Ladkrabang (KMITL) with a focus on energy optimization and path planning of mobile robotics. I have also built mobile robotics for industry. In addition, I am interested in computer vision and machine learning, and have experience in building them from research to real-world applications. I enjoy learning new software tools/frameworks and have gained extensive experience in software tools such as Pytorch, Robot Operating System (ROS), MATLAB, Node JS, and React JS.

Based on my work experience, I have been invited to work as a special lecturer at KMITL, where I teach courses such as Computer Programming, Microprocessor and Interface, Mobile Robot (Robot Operating System), Industrial Project Application, Computer Vision, and Web application & Database. My responsibilities include providing IT consultancy and being a advisor for a senior project student for the Department of Robotics and AI.

In addition to my academic work, I have several years of experience working as an application engineer. At Bombardier, I implemented interlocking software and developed autotest frameworks. I was the site specific application product owner which responsible to manage and control the software quality to accept the EN50128 for software in train control system. I experienced in Unix OS, Red Hat, Ubuntu, Centos, shell script, Python, Jenkin, Gitlab,and software quality for railway applications. During my time at Bombardier, I worked on various projects, including the MRT Purple Line project, the Durban resignalling project, the BTS Greenline extension project, and the Tehran Line 7 project.

My experience in industry also includes working as a full-stack developer for Foster Engineering Co., Ltd., which specializes in chemical mixing process control. This company provides services for large consumer goods companies such as Unilever and NEO support. In this role, I developed a web application for inventory control that interfaces with other systems such as SCADA, PLC, and SAP. Through this job, I gained experience in Microsoft SQL Server, MongoDB, React, NodeJS, Python, Docker, and VMware ESXi.
